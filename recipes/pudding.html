Pudding

Home

Description

To make a pudding, we need to mix several ingredients together first, and put them in the oven.

After this, put it in the refrigerator for a night to give it more flavor.

Ingredients

  • milk
  • whipping cream
  • egg
  • sugar

Steps

  1. cook the sugar
  2. pour the sugar to the cups
  3. boil the mixture of milk and whipping cream
  4. mix everything and bake for 40 minutes under 145 degrees
